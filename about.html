College Student

  • I am a college student in my senior year at Canisius College in Buffalo, NY.
  • I am pursuing a Bachelor of Science Degree in Digital Media Arts concentrating in Graphic Design & Web Design. In addition to my areas of concentration, my major has provided me with knowledge & experience with Motion Graphics, Advertising, Film, Interaction Design, 3D Design & Media.
  • I am also double minoring in Studio Arts & Communication Studies. Within my minors, I have gained experience with Photography, Fine Arts, Color Theory, Communication Theory & Advertising.

Graphic Designer

  • I recently completed an internship within the Creative Services department of The Buffalo News. This opportunity opened my eyes to the effects & importance of graphic design in a large company, especially within the news industry. It also provided me with wonderful opportunities to develop my skills as a designer, taught me to manage my projects in a timely fashion while maintaining quality & showed me the satisfaction of seeing my hard work appear in a widely distributed publication.
  • I have been freelancing for several years. I began experimenting with graphic design by creating projects for family & friends. Since then, I have received opportunities to test my design skills & create quality work for various clients on several scales, including local businesses, organizations & projects.
  • I love to be constantly learning & I find it exciting that there will always be knowledge to be gained.
  • I am excited to explore any opportunity to gain more knowledge for my future. I am certain of my love for design but I would like to explore a variety of opportunities to discover where I am best suited to pursue that passion.
  • As a child I wanted to be an artist when I grew up & now I’m studying to become, what I consider, the modern-day artist.

City Lover

  • I loved growing up in Buffalo & I am so excited to have watched its growth & to continue experiencing its bright future.
  • I love exploring new cities & I am inspired by anything involving urban elements, such as skylines or cityscapes. My favorite place in the world will always be New York City.
  • I am at my happiest when I am aimlessly wandering around a new city, taking in all of its beauty & observing the different lives that perfectly coexist together to give that city its character.
